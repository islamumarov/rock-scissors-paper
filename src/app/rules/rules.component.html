How to Play Rock Paper Scissors

Learn the rules of the classic game Rock Paper Scissors.

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple hand game played by two or more people. The game has three possible outcomes: rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.

To play, each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand:

  • Rock - fist
  • Paper - flat hand with fingers extended
  • Scissors: a fist with the index and middle fingers extended like a pair of scissors

After the players have chosen their shapes, they reveal them at the same time. The winner is determined by the rules:

Rules:

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Scissors beats paper
  • Paper beats rock

If both players choose the same shape, it's a tie and the game is usually played again.
